**Key Assumptions**
 - Following a major macro-event shock, macro assets follow leader-laggard dynamics with laggard assets as a result of being less competitive/actively traded
 - Beta relationships remain relatively stable during short-term windows
 - Strong event intensity represent shifts in macro regimes that impact across assets

**Limitations**
 - Leader selection is unstable in volatile regimes
 - Potential for unpredictable regime shifts in breakdowns of laggard-asset mean reversion dynamic towards leader
 - No transaction costs modelling included
 - Overlapping macro-events can breakdown stable signals
